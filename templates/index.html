Lost it? We found it!

We aim to offer a centralized and clear system for managing lost and found items on campus

Welcome to DUT Lost and Found

We are dedicated to helping students, staff, and visitors recover lost items quickly and efficiently. Our mission is to create a seamless and stress-free lost and found experience for the DUT community.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To reunite lost items with their owners quickly and efficiently.

Vision

To create a seamless and stress-free lost and found experience for the DUT community.

How It Works

1. Report

Report a lost item using our app or website.

2. Search

Search for found items in our database.

3. Claim

Claim your item securely and conveniently.

Our Location

We are located at Steve Biko Campus. Visit us to recover your lost items.
